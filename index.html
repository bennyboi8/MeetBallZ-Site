Why MeatBallZ?

We know that men aren’t always the best at talking about their health, especially when it comes to conversations that can feel awkward or uncomfortable. Our goal is to make those conversations feel more open, approachable, and easier to start through humour that encourages people to engage without the fear or stigma often attached to men’s health topics.
